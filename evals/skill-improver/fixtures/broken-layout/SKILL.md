# broken-layout — Document Helper

I will help you process documents in this skill. You can use me to do various things with files. We work together to accomplish your document goals.

## What I do

I'm a document-processing skill. I can do many things. You should use me whenever you have a document task. I will figure out what you want and do my best to help you out.

I will examine the document. You will tell me what you want. We will iterate until you are satisfied. I am very flexible and you can ask me almost anything.

## How to use me

You can invoke me by mentioning documents in your message. I will pick up the context from your conversation and act accordingly. You don't need to tell me exactly what to do — I will figure it out.

If you tell me to process a file, I will. If you tell me to validate, I will. The choice is yours.

I support various file formats, including but not limited to:
- Text files
- Markdown files
- Code files
- Configuration files

You can also point me at directories and I will process every file inside.

## Workflow

The workflow is flexible. I adapt to your needs. There are many ways to do things, and I support most of them. You can use me for:

- Quick edits
- Detailed reviews
- Bulk processing
- Single-file operations
- Whatever else you can think of

I am not opinionated about how you use me. Feel free to be creative.
